International Development Analysis (SQL)

Global Population Growth Over Time

This analysis examines global population trends using World Bank data from 1960 to 2017.

SQL was used to aggregrate population data and analyza trends over time.

Key Insights

1. Global population has steadily increased from 1960 to 2017.

2. Population growth accelerates significantly after 2000.

3. India shows the highest population growth among individual countries in the dataset.

Conclusion

The data shows consistent global population growth, highlighting increasing demand for resources and infrastructure worldwide.
